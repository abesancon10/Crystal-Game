Dog Show Game


You will be given a random number at the start of the game. This number is the number of dogs that can be entered into the dog show.

Below are pictures of the four types of dogs that are showing: Shetland Sheepdogs (Shelties), Salukis, Afghan Hounds, and Chinese Cresteds.

Each dog is assigned a random number that you cannot see. By clicking on the photo your the number of dogs entered will add up.

To win the round you must match the number of dogs entered to that of the max number of available entries.

If you enter more dogs into the show than permitted you lose the round.

At the end of each round the Available Entries, Dogs Entered, and the dogs values will reset.
